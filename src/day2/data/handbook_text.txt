# Handbook for AI Workshop

This handbook serves as a reference for participants in the AI workshop. It includes key concepts, methodologies, and resources that will be covered throughout the sessions.

## Table of Contents

1. **Introduction to AI**
   - Definition of AI
   - Types of AI: Narrow vs. General AI
   - Applications of AI in various fields

2. **Machine Learning Basics**
   - Overview of Machine Learning
   - Types of Machine Learning: Supervised, Unsupervised, Reinforcement Learning

3. **Deep Learning Fundamentals**
   - Introduction to Neural Networks
   - Understanding Layers, Activation Functions, and Loss Functions
   - Overview of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)

4. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**

   - Key Techniques: Tokenization, Lemmatization, Named Entity Recognition

5. **Computer Vision**

   - Key Techniques: Image Classification, Object Detection, Image Segmentation

6. **Ethics in AI**
   - Importance of Ethics in AI Development
   - Common Ethical Issues: Bias, Privacy, Accountability
   - Guidelines for Ethical AI Practices

7. **Hands-On Labs**
   - Lab 1: Building a Text Processing Pipeline
   - Lab 2: Fine-Tuning a Language Model
   - Lab 3: Implementing a Computer Vision Model

8. **Resources for Further Learning**
   - Recommended Books and Articles
   - Online Courses and Tutorials
   - Communities and Forums for AI Enthusiasts

## Conclusion

This handbook is designed to provide a comprehensive overview of the topics covered in the workshop. Participants are encouraged to refer to it throughout the sessions and use it as a resource for further exploration of AI concepts and practices.
